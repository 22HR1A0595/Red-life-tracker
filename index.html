About Red Life Tracker

Red Life Tracker is a web-based platform designed to connect people in need of blood with voluntary donors in their area.

Our mission is to create a simple, fast, and reliable system where:

  • Donors can register their blood group and availability
  • People with low blood levels can receive personalized diet advice
  • Medical emergencies can be responded to more effectively

Whether you're here to donate or seek help, Life Tracker is built to serve the community with compassion, accuracy, and speed.

Together, we can save lives.
