Improve Your Blood Health

If your blood level is low, your body may lack important nutrients. Follow these diet tips to improve hemoglobin levels.

  • 🍃 Iron-rich foods: Spinach, lentils, red meat
  • 🍊 Vitamin C: Oranges, citrus fruits, guava
  • 🥬 Folate: Beetroot, legumes
  • 🥤 Juices: Pomegranate, carrot juice
