Improve Your Blood Health

If your blood level is low, your body may be lacking essential nutrients. We recommend you follow a proper diet plan to restore your strength and eligibility for future donation.

  • 🍃 Iron-rich foods: Spinach, red meat, tofu, lentils
  • 🍊 Vitamin C sources: Citrus fruits, bell peppers, guava
  • 🥬 Folate boosters: Beetroot, legumes, whole grains
  • 🥤 Juices: Pomegranate, beetroot, carrot juice
